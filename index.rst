You can easily set up your UHC UCard online by visiting **activate.uhc.com** and completing a quick verification. After entering your card details and basic information, your UCard gets activated and ready for use with your health benefits.

Steps to Activate Your UHC UCard
--------------------------------

1. Open your web browser and go to **activate.uhc.com**.
2. Keep your UHC UCard handy for reference.
3. Enter your UCard number in the required field.
4. Type your Date of Birth in **MM/DD/YYYY** format.
5. Enter the last 4 digits of your Social Security Number (SSN).
6. Click on **Continue** to move ahead.
7. Sign in with your existing account, or create a new one if needed.
8. Follow the on-screen steps to verify and activate your UCard.
9. Wait for a confirmation message showing that your activation is complete.
